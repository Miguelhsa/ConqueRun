# Privacy Policy Draft

ConqueRun collects account information, profile details, foreground location while recording a run, activity statistics, group information, reports, and uploaded images.

Location is used to calculate distance, pace, route, and neighborhood conquest. It is not used for advertising.

Images uploaded for profiles and groups are reviewed before being displayed publicly.

Users can request deletion of their account and associated data from the app profile screen. A public deletion request URL must be published before App Store and Google Play submission.

Before release, replace this draft with a public, non-editable, non-PDF privacy policy URL that includes developer contact information, retention policy, Firebase, Storage, push notifications, and Strava integration details.
